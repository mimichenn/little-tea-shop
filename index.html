Our Mission

As a cafe, Little Tea Cup seeks to represent this nostalgia and foundation with high-quality ingredients and homemade items. Through elevated beverages, experience, and design we seek to inspire and uplift our local community. We hope you enjoy our Little Tea Cup!

Our Story

Our founder Mimi Chen always felt that boba shops were a unique part of Asian American culture. Growing up, she always went to boba shops as a treat with her family to indulge in after a hearty dinner. With an emotional connection to this dessert, she wanted to live out her culinary and hospitality dreams by opening a cafe of her own. As a previous barista through college, she has made it a goal to use only fresh and real ingrediants that elevates and celebrates her childhood.

Now, Mimi seeks to continue to provide a unique and high quality beverage menu, pushing creativity within Little Tea Cup and spreading the joy she felt throughout her adolescence.
